Drei Rollen: guest / registered / premium

Feature-Zugang nach Rolle:

  Feature                          Guest   Registered   Premium
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Preflop & Flop Training            ✓          ✓          ✓
  Turn & River Training              –          ✓          ✓
  Pot Odds Training                  –          ✓          ✓
  Difficulty 1 (Beginner)            ✓          ✓          ✓
  Difficulty 2 (Intermediate)        –          ✓          ✓
  Difficulty 3 (Advanced)            –          –          ✓
  Difficulty 4 (Expert)              –          –          ✓
  Leak Training                      –          –          ✓
  Fortschritt gespeichert            –          ✓          ✓

Rolle wird pro Request bestimmt:
  !session            → "guest"
  session.isPremium   → "premium"
  sonst               → "registered"
